Competitive Landscape
Where You Stand vs. AI-Enabled Competitors
Benchmarking Aerotropolis Atlanta Alliance against AI-adopting peers in Economic Development.
Risk 01
Data Privacy in Economic Intelligence
Mitigation: Implement clear data governance protocols and use only publicly available datasets. Establish partnerships with regional data providers who ensure compliance with privacy regulations.
Risk 02
Over-Reliance on AI Without Human Expertise
Mitigation: Start with AI as a research and drafting assistant, not decision maker. Require human review of all AI-generated content before external use, and invest in AI literacy training for all team members.
Risk 03
Limited Technical Resources for Implementation
Mitigation: Begin with no-code AI tools and SaaS solutions that require minimal technical setup. Partner with local tech companies or Georgia Tech students for implementation support and ongoing maintenance.
Risk 04
Stakeholder Skepticism About AI-Generated Content
Mitigation: Be transparent about AI usage and position it as enhancement, not replacement of human expertise. Create clear disclosure policies and always have human experts validate AI insights before sharing with stakeholders.
